History of the “Princess Cave Run” Development

Introduction


This is the game that is based on the arcade type, that is popular nowadays, and specifically – on that of runner. This was the first serious game which we decided to support all along and make it interesting to the maximum. And for that we could not enter the marker only with pretty graphics. Game control is out of the question as it was just like in the typical arcade. Game should be stuffed with effects, be emotionally comfortable and atmospheric. And even here development and design of the game could not be performed without the inspiration which we draw from the other game.

About the difficulties of realization I have already written in the previous article, so I will not repeat myself in many moments. We were limiting ourselves with mobile platforms and 2D design. A good arcade did not necessarily required 3D design, so our success was fully dependent from our conjoined efforts.

The mail difficulty was pass on to the designer, and from the point of view of the majority and my own, this is the most important moment of the game. Early on we throw off the importance of the unique character of the game, because it was not going to be unique in the mechanics. Work with the designer


We were basing on the good old “Tilt Monsters”. It is shown on the image below.

Vertical lane was moving and the little and the big monsters with different effects, extra points, bombs and so on appeared. The aim of the game is to score more points when dodging obstacles by moving to the left and to the right. The game looked very funny and simple. We wanted to rework this idea with new realistic design and more interesting effects. But in the total we did not manage to realize all that.

The new designer who has just started to work was incredibly talented. But up to the release of the game we could not get what we wanted to. There were lots of sketches that we needed to fit in the certain idea and atmosphere. On the practice it was not resulting well. First working game prototype we coped to make in only a few days. This was not looking so harmonically and atmospherically as we wanted it to. The example of realization can be seen on the picture below:

Charitably speaking this did not answer our interests. I can speak on graphical problems longer, but it was not the issue of designer’s potentialities, it was rather the problem of understanding. It was hard to get out a message, and frequently in such situations I wished to turn into a designer myself.

Another problem is the proper inspiration. Usually when you show to the designers works of other developers you would like to give the fantasy the right flow and create something pretty and unique. But usually such attempts did not have the proper impact. Another people (in our case - designer) took these shown games too closely and in the result of this their works were partially copied. In other words, they did not get inspired, but simply reworked what they have seen. Therefore the process of design development was always under control in order to keep it in the safe zone.


Untimely stoppage of the game and final


Currently the hero of our game is Princess and she is just running and collecting crystals, escaping from the little monsters. But we planned to animate the monsters and the heroine to the smallest details, to add lots of elements like sparkling heel, trampolines, the development of tracks and levels. In the game shop you could buy clothes for your hero, chose his style to your taste and many other.

In the end we got from the designer the approximate model of what we wanted to see. But nevertheless what we have in the game is not more than 20% of what we have planned. It is just one game level that you can endlessly pass. It is simple flow of tracks with different styles. The addition of Google LeaderBoard, Achievements, Google Ads.
