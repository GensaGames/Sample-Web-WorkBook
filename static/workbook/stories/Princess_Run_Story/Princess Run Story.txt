This is the game that is based on the arcade type, that is popular nowadays, and specifically – on that of runner. This was the first serious game which we decided to support all along and make it interesting to the maximum. And for that we could not enter the marker only with pretty graphics. Game control is out of the question as it was just like in the typical arcade. Game should be stuffed with effects, be emotionally comfortable and atmospheric. And even here development and design of the game could not be performed without the inspiration which we draw from the other game.
